# Golem Upgrades

Enhance your Modular Golems with various upgrades to improve their combat effectiveness, utility, and survivability. Upgrades are applied to golems after creation using specific items.

## How to Apply Upgrades

Drop the required item near your golem to apply the upgrade.

<Callout type="info">
	**Basic Upgrades:** Can be stacked on any iron golem. There's no limit to how
	many different basic upgrades a golem can have - mix and match freely to create
	your perfect defender!
</Callout>

<Callout type="warning">
	**Special Upgrades:** Only one special upgrade can be applied per golem. Choose
	wisely based on your needs.
</Callout>

## Tips

- Prioritize regeneration for expensive golems
- Speed upgrade essential for Netherite/Amethyst
- Farming golems don't need combat upgrades
- Name all golems to prevent accidental despawn
- Thorns synergizes well with high-health golems
