## How to Choose

- **Early Game**: Copper or Lapis for affordability
- **Defense**: Netherite or Diamond for tanking
- **Speed**: Gold for quick response
- **Special Builds**: Redstone for automation, Emerald for trading halls
- **End Game**: Super Golem for ultimate power
