# Wither's Wrath

An enhanced Wither boss fight featuring three distinct phases with unique mechanics, devastating attacks, and progressive difficulty.

## Overview

Wither's Wrath transforms the vanilla Wither into an epic boss battle with:
- **Three Progressive Phases**: Each more aggressive and unpredictable
- **Unique Attacks**: Homing skulls, charge attacks, radial blasts, and more
- **600 HP Total**: 300 hearts of pure challenge
- **Environmental Destruction**: The Wither actively reshapes the battlefield
- **Mob Summoning**: Enhanced Wither Skeletons and Blazes join the fight

## Key Features

### Phase Progression
- **Phase 1 (600-301 HP)**: Ranged combat, homing skulls, mob corruption
- **Phase 2 (300-251 HP)**: Melee combat, charge attacks, Blaze swarm
- **Phase 3 (≤100 HP)**: Enraged mode with enhanced attacks and triple dashes

### Difficulty
This boss does **massive damage**. Full enchanted Diamond armor is the minimum, and even then you'll struggle without proper preparation and strategy.
